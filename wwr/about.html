HISTORY

In 2020, after the Covid pandemic, a small group of enterpreneurs and white water rafting enthusiasts came together to commence what has grown to become, Charlee Rafting Co. It all begun as a pastime, but has now grown to be a semblance of a club of a modest 504 clientelles from 10 countries. We pride ourselves in knowing each member of our growing patrons as part of the CHARLEE family. The rafting business is our passion and we strive to ignite the same in you from your next journey with us.

Ask some of our past adventurers on a Charlee Rafting booking and you will be amazed at how much they rate us professionally and socially. We go the extra mile to please.
