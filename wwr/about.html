Whitewater Rafting Company

Rafting and whitewater rafting are outdoor entertainment activities that use a boat or an unsinkable raft.

It has different difficulty levels according to the current of the water in which the sport is done.

It can be done solo but is more commonly done with groups and needs good teamwork to manage the water.

History

In 1843 lieutenant John Fremont introduced the first rubber raft in US. Later on in 1844 the further enhancements were made by Peter Halkett.

These vessels greatly facilitated whitewater navigation. It lasted for many years, with improvements in craftsmanship and safety over time.
