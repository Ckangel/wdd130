We are poised to give you an adrenaline filled adventure on the rivers so pristine and clear. These are once in a lifetime memory and fun that will compel you to come another time soon, with your family and loved ones. We assure you of security, safety and a second to none exposure to love mother nature!

History

Our experience dates back to the very beginnings of river rafting in 1843 when Lieutenant John Fremont introduced the first rubber raft in USA, and that marked the beginning of WWR which has pioneered this adventurous river and water sport till today. Walter Helkett is also part of our successes. They conceived the idea and brought into fruition to be the very first river rafting company in America.
