    Ghana

    Ghana is located in West Africa and is sandwiched between Cote d'Ivoire (Ivory Coast) and Togo. The northern border is the country Burkina Faso and the southern border is the Gulf of Guinea. The country is slightly smaller than the state of Oregon. Ghana favors international and regional political and economic co-operation, and is an active member of the United Nations and the African Union.
